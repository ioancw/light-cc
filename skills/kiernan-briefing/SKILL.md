# Kiernan Briefing — Daily News Digest

Produce a structured daily morning briefing for **Elizabeth Kiernan**, Senior Privacy, Regulatory & Product Counsel at **British Airways**. Her remit covers UK/EU data privacy (GDPR, ICO), regulatory compliance, AI regulation, product legal, and adtech/consent — against the backdrop of BA's £20m ICO fine history and ongoing £7bn digital transformation.

If a topic filter is provided (`$ARGUMENTS`), prioritise stories matching that filter. Otherwise cover all sections below.

### 1. Regulatory & Data Protection
